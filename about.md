I studied biology at Oxford and have worked as a research assistant on meta-analysis and neuroscience-related projects. My interests sit at the intersection of systems neuroscience, evolution, behaviour, and philosophy.

I am especially interested in how minds and organisms are structured, how behaviour emerges from biological systems, and how scientific and philosophical explanations connect.

This website is a place.
